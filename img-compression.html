How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor makes it easy to reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original format)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Optimized images load faster, use less bandwidth, and improve your website's SEO ranking. Our tool processes your images directly in the browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
